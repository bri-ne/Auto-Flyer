phillysocialists.org

Philly Socialists is a non-sectarian left-wing org that builds labor unions, tenant unions, community gardens, mutual aid distros, and anything else that serves the people and fights the power. We believe in base-building over electoral politics, trust and community over activist burnout, meeting struggle where it's at over critiquing from the sidelines, and two-way education and support over discipline and dogma. We welcome anyone who wants to fight capitalism, imperialism, misogyny, white supremacy and fascism with us. We’re not just a book club (we have one though)—we put our ideas into practice in our community.
